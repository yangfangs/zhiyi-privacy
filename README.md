# 知译 隐私政策 / Zhiyi Privacy Policy

最后更新：2026-07-05

## 简体中文

**知译**（Zhiyi）是一款浏览器翻译扩展。我们高度重视你的隐私，并遵循「数据最小化」原则。

1. **不收集个人信息。** 知译没有自建的后端服务器，不收集、不存储、不上传任何个人身份信息（姓名、邮箱、账号、浏览历史等一律不收集）。
2. **配置只存本机。** 你的配置（翻译引擎、目标语言、站点规则、API Key）与翻译缓存，仅保存在你本机浏览器的存储中（`chrome.storage`），不会离开你的设备。
3. **翻译文本仅发往你选择的服务。** 为实现翻译功能，被翻译的文本会发送到你所选择的翻译服务：
   - 选择 **Bing** 引擎时，发送至微软 Bing 翻译接口；
   - 选择 **OpenAI 兼容**引擎时，发送至你自行填写的接口地址（如 DeepSeek，或运行在本机 `localhost` 的 Ollama）。

   除此之外，知译不会把你的数据发送到任何其他服务器。
4. **不出售、不共享。** 我们不会出售你的数据，也不会与任何第三方共享，更不会用于广告、信用评估等与翻译无关的用途。
5. **随时清除。** 卸载扩展即可清除本机保存的全部配置与缓存。

如对本隐私政策有任何疑问，请联系：**yangfang0227@gmail.com**

## English

**Zhiyi** is a browser translation extension. We take your privacy seriously and follow the principle of data minimization.

1. **No personal data collected.** Zhiyi has no backend server of its own. It does not collect, store, or upload any personally identifiable information (name, email, account, browsing history, etc.).
2. **Settings stay on your device.** Your settings (translation engine, target language, site rules, API key) and translation cache are stored only in your browser's local storage (`chrome.storage`) and never leave your device.
3. **Translated text goes only to the service you choose.** To perform translation, the text being translated is sent to the translation service you select:
   - With the **Bing** engine, to Microsoft's Bing translation endpoint;
   - With the **OpenAI-compatible** engine, to the endpoint URL you configure yourself (e.g. DeepSeek, or a locally-hosted Ollama on `localhost`).

   Beyond that, Zhiyi does not send your data to any other server.
4. **No selling, no sharing.** We do not sell your data or share it with any third party, and we do not use it for advertising, credit assessment, or any purpose unrelated to translation.
5. **Clear anytime.** Uninstalling the extension removes all locally stored settings and cache.
